Food

Japanese cuisine offers a wide variety of delicious and visually appealing dishes. Here are some popular Japanese foods:


Traditional dishes:
1. Sushi: vinegared rice and raw fish
2. Ramen: noodle soup
3. Tempura: fried seafood and vegetables
4. Yakitori: grilled chicken skewers
5. Tonkatsu: breaded and fried pork cutlet

Popular dishes:
1. Udon: thick wheat flour noodles
2. Soba: thin buckwheat noodles
3. Bento: boxed meal with rice, fish, and vegetables
4. Onigiri: triangular rice balls
5. Gyudon: beef bowl with rice

Desserts:
1. Mochi: rice cake
2. Manju: steamed buns
3. Daifuku: glutinous rice cake
4. Green tea ice cream
5. Yokan: traditional Japanese jellied dessert

Beverages:
1. Green tea (Sencha, Matcha)
2. Sake (Japanese rice wine)
3. Ramune (Japanese soda)
4. Mugicha (roasted barley tea)
5. Genmaicha (roasted brown rice tea)

Regional specialties:
1. Osaka: Takoyaki (octopus dumplings)
2. Kyoto: Kaiseki (multi-course meal)
3. Hokkaido: Crab, seafood
4. Tokyo: Tsukemen (dipping ramen)
5. Okinawa: Goya champuru (stir-fried bitter melon)
